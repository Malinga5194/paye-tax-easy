User Guide

System Overview

PAYE Tax Easy is a centralized PAYE tax management platform for Sri Lanka. It solves the problem of inaccurate tax deductions when employees change jobs mid-financial-year by connecting employer payroll systems with the IRD.

PortalUsersURL
🏢 Employer PortalHR / Payroll departmentshttp://localhost:5173
👤 Employee PortalIndividual employeeshttp://localhost:5174
🏛️ IRD DashboardIRD officershttp://localhost:5175
🔐 Admin PortalSystem administratorshttp://localhost:5176

Employer Portal — Workflow

  1. Go to http://localhost:5173 → click Employer Portal
  2. Login with employer@test.com / Test@1234
  3. Select a payroll period from the dropdown (April 2025 – March 2026)
  4. View the deduction summary table — all employees with annual tax, cumulative paid, remaining tax
  5. Click 📋 Fetch IRD & Report on any employee to:
    • Retrieve cumulative IRD deduction history
    • View detailed tax report popup with slab breakdown and adjustment notice
    • Download the report as a PDF
  6. Click + Add Employee to add a new employee salary record
  7. Click Finalize Period to lock the payroll period
  8. Click Submit to IRD to file the PAYE return electronically

Employee Portal — Workflow

  1. Go to http://localhost:5173 → click Employee Portal
  2. Login with any employee email (e.g. amali.fernando@test.com) / Test@1234
  3. Select financial year 2025-26
  4. View complete monthly PAYE deduction history
  5. Click ⬇ Download PDF to export tax history with the adjustment notice

IRD Dashboard — Workflow

  1. Go to http://localhost:5173 → click IRD Dashboard
  2. Login with ird@test.com / Test@1234
  3. View compliance report — total employers, submitted/not submitted, total PAYE collected
  4. Search for a specific employer by registration number
  5. Click Export CSV to download the compliance report
  6. Click Audit Logs to view the full audit trail

Admin Portal — Workflow

  1. Go to http://localhost:5173 → click Admin Portal
  2. Login with admin@payetaxeasy.lk / Admin@1234
  3. View all users across all roles
  4. Click + Create New User to add Employer, Employee, or IRD Officer accounts
  5. Activate / Deactivate users or reset passwords as needed

PAYE Tax Calculation Rules

Based on the Inland Revenue (Amendment) Act, No. 02 of 2025:

Annual IncomeTax RateTax Payable
Up to Rs. 1,800,0000%Rs. 0 (Tax Relief)
Rs. 1,800,001 – Rs. 2,800,0006%6% of amount over Rs. 1,800,000
Rs. 2,800,001 – Rs. 3,300,00018%Rs. 60,000 + 18% of amount over Rs. 2,800,000
Rs. 3,300,001 – Rs. 3,800,00024%Rs. 150,000 + 24% of amount over Rs. 3,300,000
Rs. 3,800,001 – Rs. 4,300,00030%Rs. 270,000 + 30% of amount over Rs. 3,800,000
Above Rs. 4,300,00036%Rs. 420,000 + 36% of amount over Rs. 4,300,000

Monthly deduction = Annual PAYE tax ÷ 12

Mid-year adjustment formula:

Adjusted Monthly = (Annual Tax on Projected Income − Prior Deductions) ÷ Remaining Months
